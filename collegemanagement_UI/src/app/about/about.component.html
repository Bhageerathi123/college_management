About College

shree Bhagya College for Women



  • Shree Bhagya College for Women located in heart of the city at Bangalore entrance is a landmark building with a reckoning tradition of 10 years. It is a well-known institution in Bengaluru that has been serving to empower women students and enable them to acquire skills for a better future. The College has a healthy mix of all classes and categories and caters to the educational needs of young women at an affordable cost.

    Though the college is a private and self-financing college, it has successfully and voluntarily completed three cycles of accreditation by the NAAC with ‘A’ grade in the third cycle. This has contributed to the quality enhancement of all the operations of the college.
